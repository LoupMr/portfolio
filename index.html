    Horus Project

    🔧 Description

    Horus is an AI infrastructure designed to power secure, collaborative, and self-hosted multi-agent systems. It addresses data-privacy concerns and overreliance on cloud-based AI tools.

    Horus lets organizations integrate AI while maintaining complete control over their data. It supports agentic behavior, real-time intelligence gathering, and scalable orchestration of AI agents tailored to enterprise needs.

    💡 Key Features

    • 🔄 Multi-Agent Collaboration: Agents analyze, decide, and act in coordination.
    • 🔐 Self-Hosted & Secure: Full on-premise operation, no cloud dependencies.
    • ⚡ Real-Time Intelligence: Combines RAG and Agentic AI for live, context-aware decisions.
    • 🧠 Custom AI Models: Compatible with LLaMA, Mistral, Falcon, etc.
    • 🧩 Modular & Scalable: Fits e-commerce, cybersecurity, logistics, and more.

    • Languages: Python, React, C++
